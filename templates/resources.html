Reference Papers

Before I learned about data science and advanced analytics, I learned about the mathematical concepts that make data science possible. I recently looked back at the notes on these concepts and decided to develop them into brief reference papers which can act as a resource whenever I need to remember the fundamental concepts behind the models I implement in different projects. Sometimes it’s easy to forget about the calculus and statistics that support the models we implement in our pipelines, but refreshing my understanding of the underlying mathematics helped improve my approach to training models and getting the best possible use out of them. Feel free to download and use these notes – maybe they can help you learn as well!
