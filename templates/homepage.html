Welcome!

My name is Spencer - I'm a physicist, data scientist, and lifelong learner. I built this website as a resource where I can share some of my interests, as well as the projects I’ve worked on over the years.

As a graduate student at Dartmouth, I began connecting my knowledge of machine learning with an interest in finance to work on discovering clever alternatives to traditional financial structures. I love the idea of replacing conventional (often archaic) rules with advanced analytics and understanding how the combination of different types of models can lead to new, high-performing systems.

I have degrees from Skidmore and Dartmouth in physics, mathematics, engineering, and management and my primary skillset falls into developing machine learning pipelines in Python and AWS. Feel free to explore my projects and resources to see what else I’ve been working on and thinking about lately.
